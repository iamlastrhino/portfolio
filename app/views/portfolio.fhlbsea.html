The Project

The Federal Home Loan Bank of Seattle (Now merged with FHLBDM) is a bank to provide community banks resources and loans for the local housing market.

I was the lead developer for this site coming in to accomplish a major website redesign, integrating realtime financial analysis systems. Continuing to manage iterations and support the business needs.

My Role

  • Lead Developer, Managing one other developer
  • Worked closely with business partners and design agency
  • Managing planning, estimation and budget
  • Document business rules and requirements
  • Managed compliance with Sarbanes Oxley and PII laws
  • Worked closely with IT Engineers to deploy to new data center

The Technology

  • ColdFusion, ASP, Javascript, MSSQL, Oracle Application Server, Oracle DB
  • CICD and Build - SVN, Oracle Application Server Deployment, IIS
  • Deployed to and production hosted on Bank Data Center
