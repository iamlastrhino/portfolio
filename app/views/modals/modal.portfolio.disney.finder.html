The Project

Walt Disney Parks and Resorts Online has developed a shared platform in an effort to support and serve all the theme park websites throughout the world. This portion of this effort was for the "Finder" portion of the web platform. In a quick summary, Finder is made up of the search, listing, description, maps and detail pages for all attractions, parks, resorts, hotels and events.

While I was the technical lead on this project my goals and accomplishments were to support internationalization, improve responsive/adaptive compatibility, add accesibilty compliance, improve performance and technical integration with the existing platform.

  • Lead for technical planning, estimation and budget
  • Managed integration into the Disney CICD pipeline
  • Managed security and analytics requirements and assement
  • Lead for business acceptance testing

  • Managed deployment and production integration
