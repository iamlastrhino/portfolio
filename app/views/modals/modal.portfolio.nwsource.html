The Project

NWSource.com is the local entertainment online magazine for the Seattle Times network.

The goal for NWSource was to be the hub of local information including restraunts, shows, shopping and classifieds for the Seattle area. Increasing traffic, revenue and mobile compatibility was the major focus.
