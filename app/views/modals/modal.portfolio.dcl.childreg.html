The Project

Guests that have booked a Disney Cruise and have young children are required to register their children to be eligible for activities and the nursery. This can be done once on the ship, however in attempt to get all the registration tasks completed the guests vacation we give them the opportunity to do this online.

The previous version of this application was not mobile friendly or accesibility compliant. The major goals for this project were to create an updated visual look to match the rest of the Disney Cruise websites, create a completely responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop) friendly experience and comply with accesibility standards.
